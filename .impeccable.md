# Design Context

## Users
The target audience includes users who interact with Adron Technologies, focusing on technology solutions. Their typical context involves browsing services, using digital tools, or accessing enterprise software features. The design must ensure clarity, usability, and brand consistency to support these use cases.

## Brand Personality
The Adron design system embodies:
- **Modern**: Clean, sleek elements that reflect technological advancement.
- **Professional**: A trustworthy and sophisticated style, balancing boldness with restraint.
- **Functional**: Always prioritizing usability and clarity.

## Aesthetic Direction
The visual direction prioritizes the following:
- **Typography**: Barlow for headings and Public Sans for body text (modern sans-serif).
- **Color Palette**: Teal as the primary accent, with harmonious use of dark shades (#212B36) and neutral greys. Avoid overstimulating contrasts.
- **Interactivity**: Smooth transitions and responsive feedback guided by `--transition` tokens.

### Anti-References
- No overuse of blue accents—teal is the consistent theme.
- Avoid decorative emojis; use only if explicitly requested.
- No loud, garish colors or harsh shadows.

## Design Principles
1. **Consistency**: Follow the Adron design tokens for typography, colors, and spacing as detailed in the design system.
2. **Simplicity**: Design components with ease of use in mind, avoiding clutter.
3. **Responsiveness**: Prioritize seamless adaptation across devices (see responsive breakpoints in the design system).
4. **Accessibility**: Adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA standards for contrast, clarity, and reduced motion.
5. **Delight**: Integrate subtle animations, gradients, and hover states for an engaging experience.

### Tokens Summary:
#### Typography
- `--font-display`: 'Barlow', system-ui
- `--font-body`:    'Public Sans', system-ui

#### Colors
- Primary teal: `#007373` (`--teal`)
- Background:  `#F9FAFB` (`--gray-50`)
- Borders dashed: `rgba(145,158,171,0.32)`

#### Spacing and Radius
- Corner radii: `--radius (12px)` and `--radius-lg (16px)`

#### Motion
- Transition easing: `--transition: all 0.25s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1);`
